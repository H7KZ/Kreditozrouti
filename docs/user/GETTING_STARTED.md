# Getting Started

Kreditožrouti needs to know your study context before it can show you the right courses. A short 4-step wizard handles
this on first visit. It takes about two minutes.

Open **[kreditozrouti.cz](https://kreditozrouti.cz)** — no account needed.

---

## The Setup Wizard

### Step 1 — Select your faculty

Choose the faculty where you study. This narrows the list of available study plans in the next step.

Available faculties include FIS, NF, FPH, FMV, FFÚ, FMJH, IFTG, and others. If you're unsure which faculty you belong
to, check your student ID card or InSIS → My Studies.

### Step 2 — Select your enrollment year

Choose the year you **started** studying — not the current academic year.

> **Tip:** If you're not sure, open InSIS → e-index and look at the academic year of your very first enrolled semester.

The app automatically selects the winter semester (ZS) for the chosen year as your starting point.

### Step 3 — Select your study plan(s)

Pick your field of study from the list. You can search by name or field code.

**If you have a specialization or minor:** select *both* your base study plan *and* your specialization plan. The app
merges them and shows all relevant courses.

> **Tip:** Double-click a plan to select it and proceed to the next step immediately.

### Step 4 — Mark completed courses *(optional)*

This step shows every course in your study plan. Tick the ones you've already passed — they'll be hidden from the course
list so you can focus on what's left.

This step is optional. You can skip it now and mark courses as completed later from inside the app (
see [Features Reference → Mark as completed](FEATURES.md#mark-as-completed)).

---

## After setup

Once the wizard is complete, the app:

1. Pre-filters courses to your selected study plan for the upcoming semester
2. Takes you to the course list, ready to browse and build your timetable

Your wizard selections are saved in your browser. They survive page reloads and closing the tab.

---

## Changing your setup later

- **Change plan** — the button at the top of the courses page. Resets the wizard and clears your current timetable.
- **Start over** — the link inside the wizard itself. Full reset of all selections.
