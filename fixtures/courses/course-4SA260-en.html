Course syllabus 4SA260 - Master Thesis Seminar (FIS - WS 2023/2024)

Course code: 4SA260
Course title in the language of instruction: Master Thesis Seminar
Title in Czech: Diplomový seminář
Title in English: Master Thesis Seminar
Number of ECTS credits: 3
Teaching language: english
Mode of study: full-time
Course completion: credit
Semester: WS 2023/2024
Prerequisites and co-requisites: At least 90 ECTS credits completed.
Recommended optional programme components: None.
Work placement: Not applicable.
Aims of the course:
To provide students with knowledge and skills enabling them to create a master thesis. Formulation of thesis goals, schedule and solution procedure, selection of sources. Development of the master thesis (min. 20%).
 
Learning outcomes and competences:
After successful completion students will be able to finalize their master thesis.
 
Course contents:
Explanation of the thesis writing process (formal and content requirements, academic conventions).
Setting main steps and work procedure.
Finding a suitable topic and supervisor.
Formal registration of the thesis in the information system.
 
Reading:
Basic:
Eco, U., How to Write a Thesis, MIT Press, 2015, ISBN 978-0262527132

Recommended:
Kastens, K. et al., How to Write Your Thesis, http://www.ldeo.columbia.edu/~martins/sen_sem/thesis_org.html
Bui, I. N., How to Write a Master's Thesis, SAGE Publications, 2013, ISBN 978-1452203515
